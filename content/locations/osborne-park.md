## Osborne Park's commercial hub

Osborne Park is one of Perth's densest commercial and light industrial areas. Stretching along Scarborough Beach Road, Hutton Street, and the surrounding streets, the area is home to hundreds of trade businesses, showrooms, workshops, and professional offices.

You will find kitchen and bathroom showrooms, tile suppliers, automotive workshops, electrical wholesalers, printing businesses, and a broad range of professional services — all operating within a few square kilometres. It is a working area, and the businesses here are focused on getting things done.

The proximity to the CBD and inner suburbs means Osborne Park businesses often serve customers from across the metro area. But many still rely on outdated websites — or no website at all — which means there is a clear competitive advantage for those who invest in a proper online presence.

### The showroom strip

Scarborough Beach Road is one of Perth's most recognisable commercial strips. Driving from the city towards the coast, you pass through a concentrated stretch of showrooms, trade outlets, and commercial premises that has been the backbone of Osborne Park's economy for decades.

Kitchen showrooms, bathroom display centres, tile warehouses, and flooring suppliers line the road and surrounding streets. Homeowners renovating their properties drive to Osborne Park to see products in person, but increasingly they research online first. A showroom without a strong website and good Google images is losing customers before they even get in the car.

The light industrial precinct behind the main road houses workshops, fabrication businesses, and trade operations that serve the construction and renovation industry across Perth. These businesses may not have a retail shopfront, but they still need a digital presence that communicates professionalism and capability to builders and project managers searching for suppliers.

### Close to Scarborough and the coastal suburbs

Osborne Park sits just a few kilometres from Scarborough Beach, one of Perth's most popular coastal destinations. The Scarborough Beach Road corridor connects the commercial precinct directly to the coast, and many businesses in Osborne Park draw customers from the surrounding coastal suburbs of Scarborough, Doubleview, Innaloo, and Stirling.

This proximity to high-population, high-income coastal suburbs is an advantage. Health and wellness clinics, fitness businesses, and professional services in Osborne Park serve clients who live in these suburbs and appreciate the convenient location without the premium rents of a beachside address.
