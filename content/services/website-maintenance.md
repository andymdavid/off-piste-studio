## Why ongoing maintenance matters

Your website is a business asset, and like any asset, it needs upkeep. Software gets outdated. Security vulnerabilities are discovered. Content becomes stale. Page speed degrades as browsers and devices evolve.

A website that was fast and secure at launch can become slow and vulnerable within months if it is not maintained. Search engines factor page speed, security, and fresh content into their rankings. A neglected website does not just look bad — it loses rankings, loses traffic, and loses leads.

The businesses that treat their website as an ongoing investment — keeping it updated, secure, and relevant — are the ones that consistently generate results from it.

## What is included in managed hosting

Every maintenance package includes managed hosting on fast, reliable infrastructure. You do not need to deal with a separate hosting provider, control panels, or technical server configuration. We handle all of it.

### Uptime monitoring

We monitor your website around the clock. If your site goes down or experiences an issue, we are alerted immediately and begin resolving it. Most downtime events are fixed within minutes, often before you or your customers notice.

### SSL certificates

Every site we host includes a free SSL certificate, ensuring your website loads securely over HTTPS. SSL is a ranking factor for Google and a trust signal for visitors. We manage certificate renewals automatically so your site never lapses into an insecure state.

### Daily backups

We run daily backups of your website and its content. If anything goes wrong — a bad update, accidental deletion, or a security incident — we can restore your site to a recent working state quickly. Backups are stored securely and retained for 30 days.

### Speed optimisation

We configure your hosting for performance — caching, content delivery, image optimisation, and server-level settings that keep your site loading fast. As web standards evolve, we adjust the configuration to maintain strong page speed scores.

## Content updates and changes

Your business changes over time. New services, updated pricing, staff changes, seasonal promotions — your website should reflect your business as it is today, not as it was when the site launched.

All maintenance packages include a monthly allowance for content updates. This covers changes like:

- Updating text on existing pages
- Swapping out photos or images
- Adding or editing team member profiles
- Updating business hours or contact details
- Adding new testimonials or reviews
- Seasonal banner or promotion changes

For larger changes — new pages, new features, or significant redesigns — we provide a separate quote so there are no surprises.

## Security and software updates

Websites built on any platform require regular updates. Frameworks, plugins, libraries, and server software all release patches to fix bugs and close security vulnerabilities. If these updates are not applied, your site becomes an easy target.

We handle all security and software maintenance:

- Regular updates to your site's framework and dependencies
- Security patching as vulnerabilities are disclosed
- Monitoring for malware and suspicious activity
- Firewall and access controls to block common attacks
- Immediate response if a security issue is detected

A single security breach can take your site offline, damage your reputation, and compromise customer data. Regular maintenance is the most cost-effective way to prevent that.

## Performance monitoring and reporting

We do not just host your site and hope for the best. We actively monitor performance and provide regular reports so you can see how your website is doing.

Monitoring includes:

- Page speed scores across desktop and mobile
- Uptime and availability tracking
- Traffic overview and trends
- Core Web Vitals performance
- Error detection and resolution

Depending on your package, we provide monthly or quarterly reports with clear metrics and plain-language summaries. If we spot something that needs attention — a slow page, a broken link, a drop in traffic — we flag it and take action.

## The cost of NOT maintaining your website

It is tempting to skip ongoing maintenance to save money. But the cost of neglect is almost always higher than the cost of prevention.

A hacked website can cost thousands to clean up and recover. Downtime during business hours means missed enquiries and lost revenue. A slow website drives visitors away — research shows that a one-second delay in page load time can reduce conversions by seven percent.

Then there is the SEO impact. Google favours fast, secure, regularly updated websites. A neglected site gradually loses rankings to competitors who are investing in theirs. Recovering lost rankings takes significantly more time and money than maintaining them in the first place.

For the cost of a couple of coffees a week, managed maintenance keeps your website fast, secure, and working for your business. It is one of the simplest investments you can make to protect the value of your online presence.
