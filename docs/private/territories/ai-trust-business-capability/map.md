# Territory Map: AI, Trust, and Business Capability

Status: Working map for the second topical territory.

Brief: `docs/private/territories/ai-trust-business-capability/brief.md`

Working frame: using AI to improve business capability without weakening trust.

This map turns the territory brief into an operating structure. It assigns existing content, identifies coverage strength, defines internal-linking work, and names early research priorities. It does not list article titles.

## Pillar and Hub Role

This territory should eventually have a dedicated pillar or hub page.

Current state:

- `/resources` is the public insights index, but it is not a strategic hub for this territory.
- There is no dedicated AI adoption, AI systems, or AI capability service page.
- Existing AI content mostly sits inside search visibility and AI discovery.
- Some website, design, and trust articles can support this territory, but none currently explain the broader AI capability frame.

Recommended near-term approach:

- Build the territory through one or two high-confidence clusters before creating a formal pillar.
- Use existing AI search posts only as overlap links, not as substitutes for this territory.
- Prioritise buyer problems around use case selection, trust boundaries, knowledge readiness, and customer-facing AI.
- Avoid tool-roundup content unless it supports a larger decision framework.

Future pillar role:

- Explain how businesses can use AI to improve capability, customer experience, content, workflows, and decision support.
- Act as the navigation hub for subclusters.
- Link to commercial pages where relevant, especially website design, SEO, tools, and any future AI-enabled systems offer.
- Carry the strongest models for AI opportunity selection, trust boundaries, knowledge readiness, and implementation sequencing.

## First Research Priority

Recommended first run:

Why:

- It defines the territory's decision framework.
- It helps prevent the map from becoming a tool list.
- It gives future articles a shared model for value, risk, effort, customer impact, and readiness.
- It can naturally link to every other cluster.

Research should validate:

- Buyer language around AI adoption, AI strategy, AI use cases, AI automation, and AI readiness.

- Useful source base for AI adoption, productivity, trust, risk, and organisational readiness claims.
- Commercial pathways into Off Piste's website, content, search, tools, and future AI-enabled systems work.
