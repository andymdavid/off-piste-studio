How to Use This Tool

Enter your brand colours using the six role-based inputs: primary, secondary, accent, background, text, and muted. The preview updates instantly. Switch between four website templates to see how your palette works in different contexts — a service business layout, a professional services layout, a creative portfolio, and a landing page.

Coming from the Brand Colour Extractor or Palette Generator? Your colours are pre-loaded automatically via the URL.

Why Previewing Your Palette Matters

Colours that look great as swatches don't always work on a real website. A vibrant accent might overwhelm body text. A subtle secondary might disappear against your background. This tool shows you how your palette performs in context — with headings, buttons, cards, and calls to action — so you can catch issues before you commit to a design.

Frequently asked questions

How do I preview my brand colours on a website?

Enter your brand colours in the input fields — primary, secondary, accent, background, text, and muted. The tool instantly renders them on real website mockup templates so you can see how your colour palette works in context before committing to a design.

What website templates are available?

The tool includes four template types: a service business layout (ideal for tradies and local businesses), a professional services layout (for clinics, law firms, and accountants), a creative portfolio layout, and a landing page layout. Each represents a common website style for small businesses.
