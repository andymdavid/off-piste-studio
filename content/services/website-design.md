## Why not use Squarespace, Wix, or WordPress?

Template website builders are fine for a hobby blog or a placeholder page. They are not built for businesses that depend on their website to generate leads.

The limitations add up quickly. Template builders load slowly because they carry the weight of features you will never use. They restrict your design options to what the template allows. Their SEO tools are basic. Their code is bloated. And when something breaks or you need a custom feature, you are stuck waiting on their support team or paying a developer to work around the platform's limitations.

WordPress solves some of these problems but introduces others. Plugin conflicts, security vulnerabilities, constant updates, and hosting management are all your responsibility. Most small business owners do not have time to maintain a WordPress site properly, and an unmaintained WordPress site is a liability.

We build with modern, lightweight code that loads in under two seconds. No plugins, no bloat, no maintenance headaches. Every line of code exists because your site needs it.

## What your website investment gets you

A professional website is not just a design expense. It is one of the highest-return commercial assets a service-led business can build.

Consider the maths. If your website generates five enquiries per month and you close two of them at an average job value of $1,000, that is $2,000 per month in revenue directly attributable to your website. Over a year, that is $24,000 from a one-time investment of a few thousand dollars.

Compare that to ongoing ad spend, print advertising, or directory listings that stop working the moment you stop paying. A well-built website with solid SEO foundations keeps generating leads month after month with minimal ongoing costs.

Our websites are also built to grow with your business. Need to add new service pages, target a new suburb, or launch a blog? The site architecture supports it from day one. You are not locked into a rigid template that needs to be rebuilt when your business evolves.
