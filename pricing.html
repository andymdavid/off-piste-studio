Pricing questions

How much does a small business website cost?

Our Starter package begins at $3,500 AUD for a 5-page custom website with local SEO, lead capture, and mobile-first design. Growth packages for larger sites with booking systems, blogs, and advanced SEO start from $6,500 AUD.

Are there any ongoing costs?

Your website needs hosting and occasional maintenance. Our Managed hosting plan is $49 per month and includes hosting, SSL, daily backups, uptime monitoring, and 2 content changes per month. Our Managed + Growth plan at $99 per month adds unlimited content changes, monthly SEO reporting, and priority support. Hosting is optional — you can host independently if you prefer.

What are your payment terms?

We split the build cost into two payments — 50 percent upfront to begin and 50 percent on launch. For larger projects we can arrange terms that work for both sides.

What is included in the build price?

Every build includes custom design, mobile-first development, on-page SEO foundations, lead capture forms, click-to-call, content writing if needed, and a 2 to 4 week turnaround. You get a complete, launch-ready website.

Can I host the website myself?

Yes. We can build your site and hand it over for you to host independently. Our managed hosting plans are optional but recommended — they include backups, security monitoring, SSL, and support so you do not have to manage the technical side yourself.

How accurate is the quote estimator?

The estimator gives you a ballpark range based on typical project costs. Your actual quote may differ depending on specific requirements, content complexity, and custom features. We provide a detailed fixed quote after a short discovery call.
